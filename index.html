Anvil of Apotheosis Hero Creator
Introduction
This is a tool to help create heroes from the Anvil of Apotheosis with all the restrictions baked into the options. I am in no way connected with Games Workshop or White Dwarf. The Anvil of Apotheosis is their creation and i only made this site for fun, only thing i personally added is the dawi zharr ancestry.
Information
I am working as fast as i can to add all the options, i will update the checklist when that happens. This was developed for standard 1080 screens so the site will not look alright on thin screens and on phones but i will create an app for this as well. If you find any errors or have any questions you can send me an email at chorfenjoyer@cock.li. I will continue to create similar things for tabletop games in the future, if you want to support me in my work you can give a poor student a coffee at https://www.buymeacoffee.com/chorfenjoyer ;)
